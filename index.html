Books I'm a major bookworm. Currently having a lot of fun with lower level stuff reading Hacking, the Art of Exploitation (2nd Edition) by Jon Erickson. I can't recommend this book more. It really teaches the fundamentals clearly, and covers a broad range of topics including advanced ones like Cryptology. It comes with a linux live CD based on an old version of Ubuntu, without all the modern protections (ASLR, stack canaries etc.) to help you practice the fundamentals of memory corruption before moving on to hacking more modern, safer systems. Initially I got this book just to understand buffer overflows better as they are in the OSCP, but now I'm learning so many more things that I just can't put it down!
